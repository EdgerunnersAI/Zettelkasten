# NotebookLM Changed Completely: Here's What Matters (in 2026)

> This video details the significant updates to NotebookLM, highlighting its core strengths in grounded AI answers from diverse sources, demonstrating its three-panel workflow for source management, chat interaction, and direct deliverable generation (reports, slide decks, infographics), and offering practical tips for maximizing its utility.

**Source:** [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_uXnyhrqmsU)

## Summary

## NotebookLM Overview & Core Advantage
*   **Usage & Interest**: After recent updates, NotebookLM is now more popular than Gemini in terms of usage and interest.
*   **Core Advantage**: Provides grounded answers by parsing through diverse, dense materials.
*   **Ideal Use Cases (3 conditions)**:
    *   You know which documents/files contain the answers.
    *   Sources are in different formats (PDFs, spreadsheets, slides) or mediums (text, audio, video), and no single source gives the full picture.
    *   You need the AI to stick to the document content without hallucinations, as stakes are high.

## App Layout & Workflow
*   **Simple Three-Column Layout**: Designed for a left-to-right workflow.
    *   **Sources Panel (Left)**: Where you add all documents and files (PDFs, slides, audio recordings, spreadsheets) for NotebookLM to work with.
    *   **Chat Panel (Middle)**: Where you interact with your sources by asking questions, requesting summaries, or pulling specific details.
    *   **Studio Panel (Right)**: Where you generate actual deliverables like reports and slide decks.

## Sources Panel Deep Dive
*   **Adding Existing Sources**: Start by adding documents you already possess (e.g., internal strategy documents, regional data PDFs).
*   **Discover Sources Function**: Used to find missing information.
    *   **Web + Fast Research**: 
        *   Functions like Google Search within NotebookLM.
        *   Returns a list of sources for manual review.
        *   **Rule of Thumb**: Select a maximum of three sources to act as a built-in quality filter.
        *   **Pro Tip**: Can add sources in foreign languages (e.g., Japanese) and NotebookLM will still pull relevant information and answer in English.
    *   **Drive + Fast Research**: 
        *   Functions like the Google Drive search bar.
        *   Helps find specific files in your Drive without manual digging.
    *   **Web + Deep Research**: 
        *   Finds sources and synthesizes them into a full research report, also providing a list of original sources.
        *   **Recommendation**: Not recommended for use in NotebookLM.
            *   Users with domain expertise can often filter out low-quality sources better than NotebookLM.
            *   Deep research tools in Gemini, ChatGPT, and Claude generally perform better.
*   **Source Pro Tips**:
    *   **Living Documents**: Google Docs, Slides, and Sheets added as sources are treated as living documents, allowing you to sync the latest changes.
    *   **Static Uploads**: PDFs are static uploads.
    *   **Adding Unaddable Websites**: For websites that cannot be added directly, open in reading mode, highlight all text, and paste it into NotebookLM under the "copied text" selection.

## Chat Panel Deep Dive
*   **Configure Chat Window**: The most important feature for high-stakes tasks.
    *   **Custom Instruction**: Always add a custom instruction to frame every response around your specific goal. A Gemini prompt template is available to generate this instruction.
    *   **Response Length**: Usually left on default, as you can ask NotebookLM to expand later.
*   **Managing Chat History**:
    *   **Delete Chat History**: Recommended before starting a completely new conversation to prevent the AI from being influenced by previous interactions.
    *   **Saving Information**: Useful data points can be saved as a "note"; important insights can be turned into a "source" to influence future Studio outputs.
*   **Source Guide**: 
    *   Located at the top of any source.
    *   Incredibly useful for dense sources to surface key insights and generate follow-up questions.
*   **Sponsor Workflow (HubSpot Guide)**: A free guide detailing how to run deep research in Gemini, import results into NotebookLM, and generate deliverables, broken down into 11 specific use cases (e.g., marketing strategy, competitive intelligence).

## Studio Panel Deep Dive (Production Tool)
*   **Evolution**: The Studio panel has received the most updates, transforming NotebookLM from a Q&A chatbot into a direct production tool for deliverables.
*   **Tier 1 (Must-Have) Tools**:
    *   **Reports**: 
        *   Generates finished briefing documents or competitive analyses in minutes.
        *   **Format Options**: Skips generic default formats, focusing on dynamic "suggested formats" inferred from your sources.
        *   **Custom Instructions**: Can use a Gemini prompt template to define the report's purpose and audience.
*   **Tier 2 (Situational) Tools**:
    *   **Slide Deck**: 
        *   Builds complete presentations (either "presenter slides" for visual presentation or "detailed deck" for self-contained reading).
        *   **Catch**: Downloaded PowerPoint slides are images and not easily editable elements.
        *   **Underrated Use Case**: Proposing a presentation narrative or structure to cut down brainstorming time.
        *   **Revision Feature**: Allows leaving editing instructions on individual slides to generate a new deck with applied changes.
        *   **Download Options**: PDF or PowerPoint (potential for Google Slides export in the future).
        *   **Pro Tip**: Generate vertical carousel slides (9:16 portrait format) optimized for mobile screens for social media.
    *   **Infographic**: 
        *   Turns your sources into a single polished visual for posting or sending out.
        *   **Customization**: Choose orientation (e.g., 1:1 square), visual style (e.g., instructional; avoid "detailed" for fewer text typos, "concise" recommended), and add custom prompts (e.g., specific takeaways, brand colors).
        *   **Pro Tip**: Upload a specific brand guideline as a source and instruct NotebookLM to follow it for consistent branding.
